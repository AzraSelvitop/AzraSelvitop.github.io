Who I Am

A little About Me

Hi! I'm Azra, an almost-graduate Electronic and Computer Engineering student based in Poland. I love building things at the edge of hardware and software, the layer where code makes physical stuff actually happen.

From training CNNs to read traffic signs, to wiring up a Bluetooth robot, to prototyping on an oscilloscope at 2am. I'm most comfortable when I'm figuring something out the hard way. I believe the best engineers are the ones who aren't afraid to break things.

I'm bilingual. I think in Turkish and debug in English. Currently looking for internships and interesting embedded or AI projects to sink my teeth into.
